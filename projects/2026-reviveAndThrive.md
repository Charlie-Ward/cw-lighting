Set in a slightly dingy 70s/80s sitting room. A man watches an uncanny advert promoting a product that promises to make things revive and thrive. Upon trying it on some flowers and some fried chicken it appears to work. However not is all as it seems as we see when his mother accidently eats some of the formula and gets transformed back into a young thriving woman. Finally he tries it on the ashes of his dead lover...
